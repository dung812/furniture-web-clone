ABOUT US

Welcome to Living Spaces. We are committed to making your shopping experience as easy and convenient as possible with our unmatched selection and guaranteed low prices. Best of all, you won't have to wait for your new furniture with our same day delivery! So browse and purchase online or come in to one of our showrooms to enjoy a free cup of Starbucks while experiencing the best in shopping.

Huge Selection

With over 150 Living Rooms, 115 Dining Rooms, and 100 Bedrooms, we have a huge selection of the best brands to meet any design style.

Every Day Low Prices

We're committed to offering the lowest possible prices on furniture. Our 10% Price Guarantee backs that up.Click here for full details.

Buy it Today, Enjoy it Tonight

Delivery and assembly of unlimited items for one low, flat delivery fee. Same day delivery available in certain areas for furniture purchases made before 4:00p.m.

Fun Room

We have a supervised kid's fun room where children over 42 inches tall can watch movies and play video games while their parents shop unencumbered.

Accessories

We have accessories to spice up every room in your home: lamps, plants, artwork, mirrors and many more. Stop by one of our store locations to view our full line of accessories today.

Gift Cards

With fully customizable amounts, gift cards are a great gift idea for that someone in need of some new home furnishings. Only available in store, please see a cashier for details.

Community Involvement

We have taken an active role in positively shaping the communities we're so proud to be a part of.Click here for full details.
